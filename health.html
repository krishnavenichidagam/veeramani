HEALTH CARE TECH

1. Telemedicine and Remote Care Definition: Delivery of healthcare services via digital platforms (video calls, apps). Applications: Remote consultations and diagnosis. Management of chronic diseases. Virtual mental health support. Examples: Apps like Practo, Teladoc Health, and Amwell.

2. Wearable Health Devices Purpose: Track and monitor health metrics in real time. Popular Devices: Fitness trackers (Fitbit, Garmin). Smartwatches with ECG, SpO2, and heart rate monitoring (Apple Watch, Samsung Galaxy Watch). Specialized devices for conditions like glucose monitoring (Dexcom G6).





3. Artificial Intelligence (AI) in Healthcare AI Applications: Diagnostics: AI-powered tools like IBM Watson Health assist in cancer detection. Predictive Analytics: Identifying risk factors for diseases. Medical Imaging: Enhanced accuracy in interpreting X-rays, MRIs, and CT scans. Chatbots: Virtual health assistants for symptom checking and appointment scheduling.

4. Robotics in Surgery and Care Robotic Surgery: Minimally invasive procedures with precision. Popular systems: Da Vinci Surgical System. Rehabilitation Robotics: Assisting patients with physical therapy or mobility. Elderly Care: Robots like ElliQ for companionship and reminders.
