Introduction to Space Technology

Space technology is the development and use of technologies for space exploration and travel. Space technology is technology for use in outer space. Ground-based systems for data processing and reception Space technology has many applications, including



-Weather forecasting

-Satellites can monitor the Earth's atmosphere and weather.

-Navigation

-Satellite navigation systems are used for navigation.

-Remote sensing: Satellites can gather imagery and data for crop forecasting, resource management, and environmental monitoring.

-Telecommunications

-Space technology enables telecommunications.

-Climate monitoring: Space technology can monitor the Earth's climate.

-National security: Space technology can be used for national security.
